## 🎡 The wheel, the odds and the payouts

Sitting above the wheel is the **Top Slot** — a two-reel slot that can attach a random **multiplier up to 50x** to a single bet position before the spin. Land a number and you're paid on the spot; land one of the four bonus segments and a separate bonus game begins, where far bigger multipliers are revealed, all the way to a **20,000x** ceiling on the Crazy Time round.

- The combined chance of **any bonus** is 9 of 54 segments ≈ **16.67 %**, or roughly one bonus every 6 spins across the long run.
- The **Crazy Time** segment shows up about once per 54 spins — a 1-in-54 frequency.
- **RTP** sits between **94.33 %** and **96.08 %** depending on the bet: number positions are the steadiest, bonus positions carry the highest variance.

> ⚠️ These are all **theoretical, long-term** values, and that is exactly why live stats are worth reading with a cool head. Each spin is independent — a bonus that has skipped many rounds is **not** "due", no matter what a streak looks like. The Top Slot and bonus results are RNG-certified; every chart here measures the past and forecasts nothing. Any service promising to call the next spin is misleading you.

## 🎁 The four bonus games

- **Coin Flip** — the most common bonus and the quickest: a blue coin and a red coin each carry a random multiplier, the coin is flipped, and whichever side lands face-up is paid.
- **Cash Hunt** — a giant board of 108 multipliers is hidden behind shuffled symbols; you aim at one before the timer ends, all tiles flip at once, and you take the value behind your target.
- **Pachinko** — a puck is released down a wall of pegs into a multiplier slot at the base; landing on DOUBLE doubles every value on the wall and sends the puck down again.
- **Crazy Time** — the flagship, played in a dedicated studio on a 64-segment virtual wheel with three coloured flappers. You pick a flapper, the wheel spins, and DOUBLE or TRIPLE results trigger re-spins with stacked multipliers up to the 20,000x cap.
